## Key points to cover
- Be specific and honest about each Obsidian-only syntax element rather than a vague "some formatting may not transfer" disclaimer — this specificity is the actual information gain over generic converter pages.
- Recommend a pre-export cleanup pass (resolving wiki-links to plain text, flattening callouts to blockquotes) as the practical fix, since these are genuinely Obsidian-specific extensions no standard Markdown-to-Word converter parses.
- Frame the audience correctly: this is about sharing a note with someone outside your vault (a collaborator, client, or reviewer who doesn't use Obsidian), not a general "export your vault" guide.
